The Concept

A fast-talking hot take debating game. Accumulate social currency, survive the crowd, and bankrupt your opponents in the marketplace of ideas.

Players 6 to Infinite
Ages Adults Only

Preparation

01

The Ticket

Write down 2 Hot Takes upon entry. It can be absurd, serious, or petty.

02

No "Bad Vibes"

Takes must NOT be offensive. The Host screens everything.

03

The Hat

Approved takes enter The Hat. This is the source of all chaos.

Phase I: Qualifiers

Survive the crowd. Make Top 8.

Draw a take. Defend it. But first, you have options:

The Swap

Your choice as the speaker.

  • Discard current take.
  • Draw a new one.
  • Must commit to the new take.

The Steal

The Audience's choice.

1 Person Shouts Sold!
2 People Shout Tie Breaker
3+ People Shout Void (Crash)
Thumbs Up Funny, smart, or audacious.
Thumbs Down Boring or unconvincing.

Phase II: Tournament

Top 8 Bracket. 1v1 Elimination.

Opening Statements

Player A: 25s Player B: 25s

The Free-For-All

60 Seconds of open debate.

Audience Heckling Permitted

The Verdict

Audience votes via Thumbs Up. Winner advances. Loser is out.

The Final Showdown

The last two standing. The debate format is the same, and the winner takes the whole pot!
Goal: Win the Audience Vote.

Final Verdict & Prize

  • Champion Status: The winner is decided by a final Audience Vote (Thumbs Up).
  • Prize Pool: Only the Champion receives the 1st Place Prize. The runner-up receives no prize.

Final Procedure

  1. The last two players hold a final, 60-second debate.
  2. The Host calls for the final Audience Vote (Hands Up for favorite).
  3. The votes are tallied, and the Cold Takes Champion is crowned.
